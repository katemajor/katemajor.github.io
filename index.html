 Soul Inspired Solutions for
Life's Challenging Situations

A tarot reading is a visual map of your soul at this time in your life’s journey. Tarot shows you the new opportunites that lay within the heart of any situation you find yourself in; whether it is related to issues concerning life-purpose, partner, family, career, finances, health or your spiritual path.

Every reader has their own style. Here is the style I have cultivated over the years.

I view the cards as extensions of your own wisdom.  At it's core, a reading shows you how to find the flow and harmony in all things for greater clarity, peace and ease of living.

For a reading to do its magic in your life, I will often recommend simple practices you can do at home. When a client returns and says, “I did what you suggested, and things worked out as you described them in the reading”, there is far more than fortune-telling going on. The client is experiencing an active alignment of their actions to the higher calling of their soul. In other words, they are connected with their flow.

Seeing new possibilities laid out on the table in picture form gives you the extra boost of confidence you need to take new steps.  When you change the way you do things, including the way you interpret your challenges, the parts of your life you were frustrated with or saddened by start to change as well. The internal pressure is relieved and healthy growth takes place.
